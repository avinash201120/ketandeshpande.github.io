Ketan Deshpande is the Founder and CEO of FUEL, an organization with a cumulative outreach of 1.1 million youth and a total cumulative project fund size of 20+ million USD implemented. He has received numerous awards, including Top 25 CSR Impact Leader of India and Champions of Change Award by Niti Aayog.

Recognition and achievements

  • Honored by Dr. Kalam, received Ashoka Fellowship
  • Represented India at GES-2016 at Stanford hosted by Obama.

Ketan's larger mission is to establish FUEL as a Future Skills Institute and University in Pune

Education and personal background

Ketan Deshpande holds an MBA from Symbiosis International University and completed the Entrepreneurship Program at Stanford University. He is currently pursuing his PhD in CSR at Symbiosis International University, and his family has roots in the Indian freedom movement.

Honors And Awards

Ketan Deshpande has been recognized with several prestigious honors and awards throughout his career, including being named a Top 25 CSR Impact Leader of India by India CSR and receiving the Pavate Fellowship at the University of Cambridge. He was also honored by the former President of India, Dr. Kalam, and awarded the Champions of Change Award by Niti Aayog, graced by the Hon'ble Prime Minister of India, Shri Narendra Modi Ji. Additionally, Ketan was awarded the Ashoka Fellowship in 2012 and was a semi-finalist in the Dell Social Innovation Competition at the University of Texas, Austin.
